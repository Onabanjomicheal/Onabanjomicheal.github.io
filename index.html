01 · About

Who I am.
What I build.

Michael is a Machine Learning Engineer and researcher focused on transportation systems, traffic simulation, and applied machine learning. His work sits at the intersection of urban mobility and data science, with a particular interest in African city infrastructure.

Most of his projects involve using SUMO microsimulation, reinforcement learning, and Python-based ML pipelines to study how autonomous vehicles, traffic signals, and logistics networks behave — and how they can be improved with better data and smarter models.

"Early in my career — but the problems I am working on are real, and so are the results."
